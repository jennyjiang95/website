Tangible Media

iWave

Design & Development

iWave is a novel interface to facilitate social connectedness between people not physically present in the same space. The device relies on sensors to determine the physical presence (nearness and touch) and convey that information to a remote device. An array of panels on the remote device will flutter and dance to portray a sense of another person, while lights and haptic feedback are used to convey touch.

Smilbox

Research & Development

SmilBox is a kit of paper-based tests and point of care devices that help to screen for various oral health diseases, namely caries (a.k.a. cavities), outside of the dental clinic. A low-cost diagnosis kit can assess key indicators or oral complications outside of the dental clinic could help overcome access barriers to oral healthcare, empowering individuals to engage and act in caring for their mouth.
